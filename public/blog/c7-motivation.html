How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

Currently I chase pleasure and sometimes get in the groove while coding. Actually, as a gamer, I wonder if I get into the 'flow' state while gaming. I can do it for days without getting bored!

What are your core values? What do you stand for?

My core values are equality, happiness, fairness and team spirit. I would love for there to be no conflict between gender, peoples, age groups and "social classes".

I would love to be able to own my own company and run it the way I want. I think people work too hard. I think that people with intensive, creative jobs are unproductive for much of the day because it is very hard to be productive while coding for 8 hours a day. I would require employees to work 4-6 hour days.

How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?

I recognised a lot within myself when reading this chapter. I am a very happy person and not much gets me down for a prolonged period of time. If I am upset I only need to mention my feelings to someone (for example, my wife) and I instantly become happy again. In terms of success and failure, I feel that I haven't done much that is very important, but one of the big successes of my life is finding my wife, who is an absolutely amazing person!
