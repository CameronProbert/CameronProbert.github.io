How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I focused much more on following the guidelines and really utilising the skeleton library, rather than trying to add every feature I could think of as I have done in the past. This really simplified the process of rebuilding the site. I managed to rebuild the site to look much the same as before but in much less time and with much less code.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I have always enjoyed meditating. Reading about the benefits of meditation only makes me want to practise more!

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

I didn't realise that medititation can help with concentration so much. It apparently also has many mental and physiological benefits.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

I liked the idea of sitting as if you were a mountain. I think it is easier to put yourself in a position where you feel strong and majestic rather than try to follow specific sitting instructions.

Any other musings?

After reading this, I've decided I will put aside two minutes of each day to focus on meditation. If I find I can, then I would like to increase that to ten minutes. Two minutes minimum, though.
