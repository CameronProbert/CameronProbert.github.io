How does the SCARF model apply to your Phase 0 experience so far? How will it apply to bootcamp?

I didn't have the opportunity to work with anyone during Phase 0 - or even make it to any of the check-ins, which is a shame because I feel like the other students are already forming inter-personal bonds with each other. S - Status: During Phase 0 I have been consistently improving against myself. I did not have a great deal of HTML/CSS experience before this. During the bootcamp, I will continue to do this.
C - Certainty: I have definitely felt the effects of a large amount of uncertainty during (but not related to) Phase 0. I have been extremely stressed because of it and definitely felt as though my decision making skills were hampered because of it.
A - Autonomy: After taking mitigating steps against the uncertainty that was stressing me, I felt much better. I'm glad I had the autonomy to be able to take these steps. I agree with Meng's statement that it's the "helplessness in the face of stress" that actually causes bad feelings.
R - Relatedness: As I wrote earlier, I haven't actually made it to any check-ins for my cohort, and so I haven't met any of them. However, I met and talked to Jack and Harrison from EDA and when I've had to talk to them they have been very nice, friendly, and relatable and that gives me the confidence to ask for anything I need to help me with Phase 0 or EDA in general.
F - Fairness: Fairness has not had a big impact on my Phase 0. I have been doing everything alone so I've not had to compromise with anyone else. Harrison at one point 'suggested' a deadline to complete up to Sprint 7 before which was very fair (in fact, I'm completing Sprint 9 now, well before the end of February, which was the deadline).

Have a difficult conversation. How did it go? (It's a valuable skill, but can take practice to develop!)

I talked to my wife about something that had been on my mind recently. She is a very empathetic person and was only supportive of me, and then she also raised some things that had been on her mind too. I think it really helped the both of us to share with each other things that had been stressing us and we know that we both have very suppoortive partners in each other.
