Time and habits

After reading about time management as part of EDA Phase 0, I have decided I will try the Pomodoro Technique. I notice that Toggl has this functionality built in.

I find it interesting that many guides that talk about time management almost focus more on break times than productive times. I guess that if you work solidly for a long period of time then you burn out, and you can't really call that 'time management'.

Update: I have been trying to use the Pomodoro Technique and have been enjoying it so far. I have found that I can work on a problem without interruption or distraction for 25 minutes and the break time lets me do things that I have been thinking about while working and get them out of my system.

which will you experiment further with? I'm going to keep using the Pomodoro technique for the foreseeable future. I am sure I will only get better at it as I continue using it.
