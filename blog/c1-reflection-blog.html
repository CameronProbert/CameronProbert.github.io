First Blog

Having just started Enspiral Dev Academy's (EDA) phase 0, I am very excited to polish my skills as a web developer. I think EDA offers a very balanced perspective to working in the technology industry. I am particularly interested in doing things related to mindfulness and keeping a balanced work and private life.

I would like to fully engage with my team-mates during my time with EDA. I have prior programming experience from my Bachelors of Science with computer science and I tutored students after my first year studying. Because of this I'm ready to help my cohort and I'm ready learn from them because everyone is good at different things.

I chose to study with EDA because of the way they marketed themselves - as a course that teaches up to date web development skills, with a very high graduate employment rate in the field. I still expect this from them, and I am ready to commit myself to this. I have been especially impressed talking to the EDA staff about their positions on student learning (highly collaborative) and social equality and diversity.

Working in the highly collaborative environment of EDA makes me nervous as I will have to rely on others to produce work, and I have been disappointed by others in the past. However, each new team deserves to be trusted until proven otherwise and I'm looking to learn a lot from my new team-mates as well as other skills like leadership and planning.
